### 1. Information You Provide Directly

#### Account Information
- **Google Sign-In**: If you choose to sign in with Google, we collect your Google account email, display name, and profile picture to identify your account and enable cloud save features.
- **Google Play Games Services**: If you're on Android, we automatically collect your Play Games player ID to enable leaderboards, achievements, and multiplayer features.

#### User-Generated Content
- **Display Names**: Any custom display names you set in the game.
- **Multiplayer Data**: Room codes, game moves, and chat messages when participating in multiplayer sessions.

### 2. Information Collected Automatically

#### Game Progress Data
- Level completion status, scores, stars earned, attempts made
- Achievement unlocks and daily challenge completions

- Theme and customization preferences
- Sound and notification settings

#### Device Information
- Device model, operating system version
- App version and build number
- Device identifiers (for anonymous analytics and authentication)

#### Usage Information
- Game session duration and frequency
- Feature usage patterns (which game modes you play)
- In-app purchase history
- Ad interaction data (when you view or interact with ads)

#### Technical Data
- Crash logs and error reports
- Performance metrics (loading times, frame rates)
- Network connectivity status

## Data Storage and Security

### Cloud Storage
- Your game progress is stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore when you sign in
- Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
- Data at rest is encrypted by Firebase's infrastructure

### Local Storage
- Game preferences and cached data are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences
- Local data is protected by your device's security features

### Data Retention
- Active account data is retained for the duration of your account's existence
- Inactive accounts (no sign-in for 24 consecutive months) may have cloud data deleted without notice
- Local device data remains until App uninstallation
- Backup copies may persist in our systems for up to 90 days after deletion
- We reserve the right to retain certain data as required by law or legitimate business interests

**RETENTION DISCLAIMER**: We make no guarantees regarding data preservation. ## Your Privacy Rights and Choices

### Access and Control
- **View Your Data**: You can view your game progress, achievements, and statistics within the app
- **Export Data**: Contact us to request a copy of your data
- **Delete Data**: Reset your game progress through Settings → Reset All Game Data
- **Delete Account**: Contact us to request account deletion

### Communication Preferences
- **Notifications**: Toggle notifications on/off in the Settings screen
- **Marketing**: We don't send marketing emails (we communicate only through in-app notifications)

### Advertising Choices
- **Personalized Ads**: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings:
  - **Android**: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

### Google Account
- **Sign Out**: You can sign out of your Google account at any time through Settings
- **Revoke Access**: Revoke app permissions through your Google Account settings
